Frequently Asked Questions

How does CaseLift work?
Your TC records a consultation in CaseLift. CaseLift analyzes the recording, identifies objections, reads exit intent, and automatically sends a personalized follow-up sequence, SMS and email, until the patient schedules. When they reply, your TC takes over and closes.
Does CaseLift only work for implants?
No. CaseLift recovers cases for any high-value dental treatment, implants, full arch, Invisalign, cosmetic, sleep apnea, full mouth rehab. If a patient left without scheduling, CaseLift follows up until they do.
What if our practice does a mix of treatments?
That's exactly who CaseLift is built for. CaseLift handles your entire high-value case mix from one platform, adapting its analysis and follow-up to each specific treatment type automatically.
Is CaseLift HIPAA compliant?
Yes. CaseLift is fully HIPAA compliant. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We sign a Business Associate Agreement with every practice before you record a single consult.
How does CaseLift know what to say in follow-ups?
CaseLift analyzes the actual recording. It identifies the patient's specific objections, personal details, and readiness to move forward, then writes messages that reference their exact situation. Every message sounds like it came from your practice.
Do I need special hardware?
No. CaseLift records directly in your browser. If you use Doxy.me for virtual consults or a Plaud device, CaseLift integrates with those too.
What is The Conversion Collective?
The Conversion Collective is the private community included with every CaseLift membership. Dental practices share what's working, get coaching from TC specialists, and support each other. Laura Calle, a certified implant TC, runs it and hosts monthly group coaching calls.
What is the guarantee?
If CaseLift doesn't return at least 3X what you pay within your first 90 days, your membership is free until it does. Recovery is tracked and verified against your real PMS production data, not our word for it. And you can cancel anytime, there's no long-term contract.
How is CaseLift different from other dental software?
Most dental software tracks what happened. CaseLift changes what happens next. It doesn't just show you unconverted patients, it follows up with them, brings them back into your schedule, and tracks every dollar recovered. No other platform does this.
